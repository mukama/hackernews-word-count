HackerNews Word Count

Endpoints

1. /api/v1/common-in-titles
Returns the top 25 most occuring words from the titles of the last 250 posted stories and the corresponding amount of times each of these top words appears.

2. /api/v1/common-in-comments
From the last 1000 items currently available via the hackernews API, selects only the comments texts and returns the top 25 most occuring words and their corresponding number of times they occur.
